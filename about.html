Our Vision

Bridge Addis was founded with a single mission: to ensure that every university student in Addis Ababa graduates with more than just a degree. We believe in the power of real-world experience.

The Problem

Many students in Ethiopia focus heavily on theoretical knowledge. Meanwhile, local small businesses and NGOs need fresh talent but lack a direct way to find students. We bridge that gap.

How It Works

  • For Students: Build a profile and apply to local opportunities.
  • For Partners: List internships and find capable youth to enhance productivity.
